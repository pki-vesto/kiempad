# Fertility Intelligence Strategic Domain

Kiempad is not merely a fertility tracker. Kiempad must evolve into a personal
fertility intelligence platform that helps users centralize, understand and prepare
around their fertility journey without becoming a medical device.

This domain is a permanent source of active goals. The goal catalog must maintain:

- At least 25 active goals related to Fertility Intelligence.
- At least 5 active goals related to Research Intelligence.
- At least 5 active goals related to Daily Recommendations.

When these thresholds are not met, generate additional goals before unrelated roadmap
expansion. The active goals must preserve Kiempad's boundaries:

- No diagnosis.
- No dosage advice.
- No treatment-choice advice.
- No plaintext medical content on the backend.
- Explicit opt-in for network, AI and research aggregation.

## Scope

### Historical Medical Intelligence

Generate goals around medical document ingestion, historical record import, OCR,
timeline reconstruction, metadata extraction and fertility dossier creation.
Supported inputs include lab reports, fertility investigations, clinic reports, PDFs
and scanned documents.

### Imaging Intelligence

Generate goals around ultrasound uploads, photo uploads, scan uploads, embryo image
uploads, image classification, image comparison and image timeline tracking.

### Consultation Intelligence

Generate goals around consultation uploads, transcript analysis, consultation
summaries, action extraction and treatment-context preparation. Treatment
recommendations must stay limited to non-advisory preparation and questions for the
clinician.

### Embryo Intelligence

Generate goals around embryo tracking, embryo grading, embryo history, embryo
comparison and embryo outcome tracking. Grading is recorded as clinic-provided source
language, not as Kiempad scoring or selection advice.

### Research Intelligence

Generate goals around fertility research aggregation, literature discovery, evidence
summaries, trend detection and research relevance scoring.

Every research item should support:

- Scientific summary.
- Patient-friendly summary.
- Personal relevance explanation.

### Daily Recommendations

Generate goals around female and male recommendations for nutrition, supplements,
cycle support, treatment preparation, fertility optimization and lifestyle
improvement. Recommendations should become increasingly personalized over time while
remaining explainable and non-medical.

### Fertility Knowledge Graph

Generate goals around relationship mapping, treatment history, research linkage,
embryo linkage, consultation linkage and recommendation linkage.

### Fertility Timeline

Generate goals around complete fertility journey visualization, treatment timeline,
embryo timeline, consultation timeline, recommendation timeline and research timeline.
